Pokémon Gold/Silver Restoration v1.3.6 by Masaru
https://github.com/Masaru2/pokegold-r

This patch reverts most of the changes between the japanese and the english version of Gold and Silver
It also serves as an update for the romhacks made by NikcDC/USC in 2011

More info about the regional differences:
https://tcrf.net/Pok%C3%A9mon_Gold_and_Silver/Version_Differences
https://tcrf.net/Proto:Pok%C3%A9mon_Gold_and_Silver/Localization_Prototypes/English

Features:
-Japanese GFX backported (ex. PC/Shop, Trainer, Pokémon Sprites)
-Phanpy/Donphan and Teddiursa/Ursaring locations reverted to what it was in the japanese version
-Restored trainer card names
-Olivine Lighthouse now has its full name on the map (It was shortened due to a text limit glitch)
-"Miracle Cycle" references restored
-Bugs that wasn't in the japanese version fixed (ex. Coin Case glitch, text overflow)
-Debug mode from the localization builds added as a optional patch
